## Key Success Principles

1. **Understand the System First** - Master the basics before trading real money
2. **Risk Management is #1** - Protect your capital at all costs
3. **Psychology is Critical** - Your mindset determines your success
4. **Paper Trade First** - Practice with virtual money until perfect
5. **Keep a Journal** - Track all trades and learn from them
6. **Follow Your Plan** - Discipline over emotion always wins
7. **Never Stop Learning** - Markets evolve, so must you
8. **Start Small** - Risk small amounts as you learn
9. **Be Patient** - Consistency builds wealth over time
10. **Accept Losses** - They are part of trading, not failure
